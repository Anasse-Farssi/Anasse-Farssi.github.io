"RoBERTa and Vader Models in Sentiment Analysis of Spotify Reviews"

Introduction:

Sentiment analysis has gained significant importance in the field of natural language processing. In this project, we aimed to perform sentiment analysis on the Spotify reviews dataset and compare the performance of two different models, namely Vader and Roberta, for predicting the sentiment of the reviews.

Methods:

We started by importing the necessary libraries and loading the Spotify reviews dataset. Next, we generated the Vader score for each review, which represents the compound sentiment score ranging from -1 (most negative) to 1 (most positive), and plotted the distribution of the scores. After that, we preprocessed the text data and used the Roberta model from Hugging Face for generating sentiment scores, utilizing a GPU for faster performance. The sentiment scores generated by the Roberta model were also plotted and compared with the Vader scores.
