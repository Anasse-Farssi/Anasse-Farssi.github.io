" Marketing Analytics and Power BI Dashboard"

Introduction:

The aim of this project was to identify the top-performing stores for expansion based on their revenue and marketing spend. The return on investment (ROI) was calculated using the formula (revenue - spend) / spend. Additionally, we conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify unique states, cities, regions, and new expansions in the dataset.

Methods:

The first step was to calculate the ROI for each store and then extract data for EDA, including unique states, cities, regions, and new expansions. After that, we checked for any missing or duplicated data in the dataset.
In the analysis stage, we plotted the count of the first region against the second region to identify which region had a higher count in the data. We also calculated the ROI for each region and by state for each region. We compared the ROI for old and new expansions and selected the top five stores with the highest profit for each region.

Results:

The ROI, ROAS, and net profit were calculated to support our insights. The findings of this study can be used to make informed decisions regarding store expansion, and the interactive Power BI dashboard created can provide insights related to states and regions as well as the recommended stores for expansion.
